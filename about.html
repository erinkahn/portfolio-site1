about

Erin Kahn is a graphic/web designer and front-end web developer from Birmingham, Alabama. She is passionate about learning, problem solving and self-improvement. Erin has a reputation of being diligent in everything she does.

In college, she studied advertising and graphic design at The University of Alabama. Now, she spends most of her time at The Creative Circus, where she studies web development and tightrope walking... When she's not coding, Erin can be found hiking on trails, painting on her balcony, FaceTiming her dog Lilly, trying new breweries in Atlanta and completing items on her bucket list.
